# Journal Option: Machine Learning: Science and Technology (MLST) Focused Collection

We are excited to announce a **special publication opportunity** for ICML 2026 AI for Science Workshop participants! In partnership with *Machine Learning: Science and Technology (MLST)*, we have organized a **focused collection** dedicated to advancing AI for scientific discovery.

## 📚 Focused Collection: AI for Scientific Discovery

This collection provides a fast-track publication venue for high-quality research presented at our workshop. Selected papers will undergo rigorous peer review and be published in a dedicated collection on the IOP Science platform.

**Collection Link:** [**Machine Learning: Science and Technology – AI for Scientific Discovery**](https://iopscience.iop.org/collections/mlst-260607-1156)

## 🎯 Scope

We welcome submissions that align with the workshop's themes, including but not limited to:

- **AI Scientists as Tools, Co-authors, and Founders** – Systems that assist, collaborate, or autonomously drive scientific discovery
- **Verification and Validation** – Methods for verifying AI-generated hypotheses, simulations, and experimental results
- **Closed-Loop AI Scientists** – Autonomous systems that integrate hypothesis generation, experimentation, and iteration
- **Domain-Specific AI for Science** – Applications in physics, chemistry, biology, climate science, materials science, and beyond
- **Benchmarks and Datasets** – New resources for evaluating AI scientists and their components
- **Sociotechnical Aspects** – Governance, ethics, and responsible deployment of AI scientists in real-world labs

## 📝 Submission Guidelines

### For Workshop Participants
- Authors of **accepted workshop papers** are encouraged to expand their work for submission to the MLST focused collection.
- New submissions that align with the scope are also welcome.

### Manuscript Requirements
- Follow the [MLST author guidelines](https://iopscience.iop.org/journal/2632-2153/page/Author%20guidelines)
- Select **"AI for Scientific Discovery"** as the article type when submitting
- Clearly indicate in your cover letter that the work is related to the ICML 2026 AI for Science Workshop

### Review Process
- All submissions undergo **peer review** according to MLST's standards
- The focused collection ensures visibility alongside other cutting-edge AI4Science work
- Fast-track processing for high-quality workshop papers

## 🏆 Benefits of Publishing in the Focused Collection

1. **Visibility** – Your work appears in a dedicated collection alongside other leading AI4Science research
2. **Rigorous Review** – Benefit from MLST's established peer review process
3. **Open Access** – IOP Science provides wide dissemination of your work
4. **Community Building** – Contribute to a centralized resource for the AI4Science community
